Trip Monitoring SOP

1. Delayed Trip:
A trip is delayed if onward travel duration is above the configured threshold.

2. Geofence Miss:
A trip is geofence miss when the vehicle does not hit the configured unloading/customer geofence.

3. Data Loss / EPF:
EPF means external power failure or device power disconnection. Data loss means GPS packets are missing or tracking quality is poor.

4. NDD:
NDD means no destination detection. It can happen due to GPS loss, wrong geofence, or incomplete unloading event capture.

5. Recommended Action:
- Delay: validate route, unloading TAT, customer hold, transporter issue.
- Geofence Miss: validate geofence coordinates and radius.
- Data Loss: check device wiring, power supply, GPS health.
- NDD: validate actual unloading location and trip event continuity.
